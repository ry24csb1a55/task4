About Jaipur

Jaipur, famously known as the Pink City, is a blend of tradition, culture, and history. It is the capital of Rajasthan, known for its stunning palaces, colorful markets, and historic landmarks. From the majestic Amber Fort to the intricate architecture of Hawa Mahal, Jaipur offers a glimpse into India's royal past.

Top Attractions

City Palace

A grand palace complex that reflects a blend of Rajput and Mughal architecture.

Hawa Mahal

The iconic Palace of Winds, with its unique facade designed for royal ladies to observe street life.

Amber Fort

A stunning fort located on a hilltop, showcasing Rajput architecture and scenic views.

Jal Mahal

A Beautiful Palace Surrounded by the Calm Waters of History.

Nahargarh fort

Known for its breathtaking views of the city, this fort is a popular sunset spot.
